STUDY AREA: Foping National Nature Reserve, Qinling Mountains, Shaanxi
  Province: 陕西 (Shaanxi), Hanzhong, Foping County
  Centroid coords: ~33.6°N, 108.0°E (Qinling core giant panda habitat)
  Elevation: montane forest (giant panda habitat belt)

SAMPLING:
  Camera traps: 130 (on 57 transects)
  Survey period: 2015-01 to 2017-12 (3 years)
  Camera-days: 101,220
  Independent images: 36,100 (30,563 mammals + 3,244 birds)
  Species: 29 wild mammals (15 families, 6 orders) + 54 birds (14 fam, 8 orders)
